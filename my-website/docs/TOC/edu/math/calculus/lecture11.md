# Inverse Functions #


- $\ln(yY)  = \ln y + \ln Y$

<br/>

<div style={{display: 'flex', justifyContent: 'left'}}>

$$
\begin{cases}
y = e^x \\
Y = e^X 
\end{cases} \\

\begin{aligned} \\
yY &= e^{x + X} \\
\ln (yY) &= x + X = \ln y + \ln Y
\end{aligned}
$$

</div>
